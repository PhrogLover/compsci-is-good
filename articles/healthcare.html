As we progress into the future, Computer Science is playing a bigger and bigger part in the field of healthcare. From medical informatics to AI based diagnostics, computer science is shaping the landscape of the medical world.

Today with the aid of powerful computer and information networks, hospital can easily organize and manage data records of patients, biomedical research and education. Gone are the days of sifting through paperwork to find a single slip of paper, now doctors can with the click of a mouse retrieve years worth data on a patient allowing for more effective medical practices.
